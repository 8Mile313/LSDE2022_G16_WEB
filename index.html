W1: Covid-19 on the Web

LSDE2022 Group16: Weiqiang Guo, Lu Wang, Zheng Zhang

About

Along with our primary focus on the UK, we also spent additional time working with the pages of other designated nations that were included in the common crawl. The dynamic map will show the public's interest in coronavirus with a timeline by counting the number of pertinent pages in each month.

The word cloud is generated based on the YAKE! model by extracting keywords from each relevant page and weighting them with the word frequency of the month. The larger the area in the word cloud, the more frequently the word appears. Click on a keyword in the word cloud to jump to its related page.

To obtain the sentiment analysis results, two steps are required. Based on the results from the T5-Transformer summary page, where Bert's sentiment classification model divides the contents into sentiment categories and displays the weighting of positive and negative sentiment for the month.
